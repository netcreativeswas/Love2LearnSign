# Privacy Policy for Love to Learn Sign

**Last updated:** 27 December 2025

**App Developer:** NetCreatif

**App Name:** Love to Learn Sign

**Package Name:** com.lovetolearnsign.app

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Love to Learn Sign. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address (for account creation and sign-in)
- **Profile information**: Display name
- **Demographic information (optional)**: Hearing status (hearing person / hearing impaired)
- **Country selection** (for onboarding and app experience)
- **Language preferences** (English/Bengali selection)
- **Quiz settings** (time limits, question counts)
- **Favorites** (saved words and signs)
- **Quiz history** (completed quizzes and scores)
- **Notification preferences** (daily reminder settings)
- **Subscription preferences** (premium subscription choices)
- **Optional notes** you choose to provide during signup (free-text)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device information** (operating system, device model)
- **App usage data** (features used, time spent, video views)
- **Performance data** (app crashes, errors)
- **Cache data** (downloaded video content for offline viewing)
- **Video view counters** (to determine when to show advertisements)
- **Game session data** (flashcard and quiz session usage)
- **Ad interaction data** (collected by Google AdMob - see Third-Party Services)
- **Dictionary search analytics** (sanitized query text, timestamp, category, result count, found/missing flag, anonymous session ID)

### 2.3 Account and Authentication Data
When you create an account or sign in:
- **Email address** (stored securely via Firebase Authentication)
- **User ID** (unique identifier assigned by Firebase)
- **Email verification status**
- **User account status** (pending approval, approved, etc.)
- **User roles and permissions** (e.g., paidUser, admin, editor)

### 2.4 Premium Subscription Data
If you subscribe to Premium:
- **Subscription type** (monthly or yearly)
- **Subscription start and renewal dates**
- **Subscription platform** (Android or iOS)
- **Payment information** (processed securely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store - we do not store payment details)
- **Subscription status** (active/inactive)

### 2.5 Permissions Required
Our app requests the following permissions:
- **Internet Access** - Required for video streaming, data synchronization, and advertisements
- **Post Notifications** - Required for daily learning reminders
- **Schedule Exact Alarms** - Required for precise notification timing

### 2.6 Search Analytics (Anonymous)
- We log each dictionary search with the sanitized query text, timestamp, selected language/category, number of results, whether the search returned any matches, and an anonymous session ID generated when the app starts.
- Search analytics do **not** store your email, user ID, or any other personal identifier.
- The data is used in aggregate to identify missing words, prioritize new content, and improve search quality.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app service
- Authenticate and manage user accounts
- Personalize your learning experience
- Send daily reminder notifications
- Manage premium subscriptions and access
- Display advertisements (for non-premium users)
- Track video views and game sessions to determine ad frequency
- Analyze anonymous search analytics to identify missing words and improve dictionary relevance
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- Store video content locally for offline access
- Process in-app purchases securely through platform stores

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Firebase Services
Our app uses Google Firebase services:
- **Firebase Authentication** - Manages user accounts and authentication
- **Firestore Database** - Stores sign language content, user preferences, subscription data, and usage counters
- **Firestore Search Analytics** - Stores anonymous dictionary search entries (query text, timestamps, category, result count, found flag, session ID, no user IDs)
- **Firebase Hosting** - Serves video content
- **Firebase Cloud Functions** - Processes subscription updates and user roles

### 4.2 Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
Our app uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements:
- **Interstitial Ads** - Shown after viewing 8 dictionary videos
- **Rewarded Ads** - Shown to unlock additional game sessions
- **Ad Interaction Data** - Google AdMob collects data about ad views and interactions for advertising purposes
- **Advertising ID** - Used by AdMob to personalize ads (you can reset this in your device settings)
- For more information about how Google uses data, visit: `https://policies.google.com/privacy`

### 4.3 In-App Purchase Services
Premium subscriptions are processed through:
- **Google Play Billing** (Android) - Handles payment processing for Android users
- **App Store In-App Purchase** (iOS) - Handles payment processing for iOS users
- We do not store or have access to your payment card information
- Payment data is processed securely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store

### 4.4 Video Content Delivery
- Videos are streamed from our content delivery network
- Videos are cached locally for offline viewing

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

### 5.1 Local Storage
- Settings, favorites, and videos are stored locally on your device
- Video view counters and session data may be stored locally for guest users

### 5.2 Cloud Storage
- User account information (email, roles, status) is stored securely in Firebase
- Subscription data is stored in Firebase Firestore
- Usage counters (video views, game sessions) are stored in Firebase Firestore
- Anonymous search analytics entries (query text, timestamps, result counts, session IDs) are stored in Firebase Firestore to improve content coverage
- Preferences may be synced securely via Firebase across your devices

### 5.3 Retention
- **Local data**: Removed when you uninstall the app
- **Cloud data**: 
  - Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  - Subscription data: Retained for legal and accounting purposes as required by law
  - Usage counters: Retained to provide app functionality
  - You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at info@netcreative-swas.net

## 6. User Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data through the app settings
- **Modify** your account information and preferences
- **Delete** your account and associated data (contact us to request deletion)
- **Control** notification and language preferences
- **Opt-out** of personalized ads by resetting your Advertising ID in device settings
- **Cancel** your premium subscription at any time through Google Play Store or App Store settings

## 7. Premium Subscription

- Premium subscriptions remove all advertisements
- Premium users have unlimited access to all features
- Subscription payments are processed securely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store
- We do not store your payment card information
- Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled
- You can manage or cancel your subscription through your device's store settings

## 8. Advertising

- Non-premium users will see advertisements:
  - Interstitial ads appear after viewing 8 dictionary videos
  - Rewarded ads are available to unlock additional game sessions
- Premium users do not see any advertisements
- Ad personalization uses your Advertising ID (can be reset in device settings)
- Google AdMob may collect data for advertising purposes (see Google's Privacy Policy)

## 9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at info@netcreative-swas.net and we will delete the information.

## 10. International Use

Data may be stored in countries with different data protection laws. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your data to these countries.
