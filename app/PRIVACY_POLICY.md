# Privacy Policy for Love to Learn Sign

**Last updated:** 29th August 2025

**App Developer:** NetCreatif

**App Name:** Love to Learn Sign

**Package Name:** com.lovetolearnsign.app

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Love to Learn Sign. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Language preferences** (English/Bengali selection)
- **Quiz settings** (time limits, question counts)
- **Favorites** (saved words and signs)
- **Quiz history** (completed quizzes and scores)
- **Notification preferences** (daily reminder settings)

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device information** (operating system, device model)
- **App usage data** (features used, time spent)
- **Performance data** (app crashes, errors)
- **Cache data** (downloaded video content for offline viewing)

### 2.3 Permissions Required
Our app requests the following permissions:
- **Internet Access** - Required for video streaming and data synchronization
- **Post Notifications** - Required for daily learning reminders
- **Schedule Exact Alarms** - Required for precise notification timing

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the app service
- Personalize your learning experience
- Send daily reminder notifications
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
- Store video content locally for offline access

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Firebase Services
Our app uses Google Firebase services:
- **Firestore Database** - Stores sign language content and user preferences
- **Firebase Analytics** - Provides app usage insights (if enabled)
- **Firebase Hosting** - Serves video content

### 4.2 Video Content Delivery
- Videos are streamed from our content delivery network
- Content is cached locally on your device for offline viewing
- No personal data is transmitted during video streaming

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Local Storage
- Quiz preferences and history are stored locally on your device
- Video content is cached locally for offline access
- Favorites and settings are stored using secure local storage

### 5.2 Cloud Storage
- User preferences and quiz history may be synchronized across devices
- Data is stored securely using Firebase's security features
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS

## 6. Data Retention

- **Local data** (preferences, cache) is retained until you uninstall the app
- **Cloud data** (if synced) is retained until you request deletion
- **Video cache** is automatically managed and may be cleared based on device storage

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data stored in the app
- **Modify** your preferences and settings
- **Delete** your data by uninstalling the app
- **Opt-out** of notifications through device settings
- **Control** language preferences and app settings

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## 9. International Users

If you are using our app from outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and stored in countries with different data protection laws.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last updated" date
- Sending a notification through the app (if applicable)
